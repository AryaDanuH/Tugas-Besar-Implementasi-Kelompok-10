Libmatch Privacy Policy

Diperbarui: 13 Januari 2025

1. Who we are

We ("LibMatch," "we," "us," or "our") operate the LibMatch website and mobile app (collectively, the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we store, use, share, and protect it.

2. Information we collect

Information you provide:

  • **Account registration name, email, phone number, password** (hashed).
  • **User display name, profile photo** (optional).
  • **Addresses** for book delivery and borrowing.
  • **Payment information** (processed by payment providers, we do not store full card numbers).
  • **Your book wish lists and borrowing/return history.**
  • **Support communications:** emails, chat logs, and feedback.

Information collected automatically:

  • **Device and usage information:** OS, app version, IP address, browser, pages/screens visited, and timestamps.
  • **Geolocation:** we may collect location information (IP location data) if you grant permission (used for branch suggestions or pickup features).
  • **Cookies & similar technologies** (if enabled): We use cookies and similar technologies to provide features, remember preferences, analyze usage, and allow basic personalization. You can manage cookie settings in your browser or within the app.

3. Definitions

For convenience, these terms define a few capitalized words used in these Terms:

  • "Book" or "Books": physical or digital books, comics, magazines, or other reading materials offered for loan or borrowing.
  • "Borrowing": the transaction by which a User borrows a Book via Pick-up or Delivery.
  • "User Content": content submitted by Users to the Services, including reviews, comments, photos, profile information, and other uploads.

4. Sharing & disclosure

We only share information with:

  • **Service providers (payment processors, delivery partners, analytics providers)** who process data on our behalf.
  • **LibMatch partners** (libraries or trading partners) for returns or resolve issues.
  • **Law enforcement or regulation** if required by law or to protect rights.
  • **With your consent** for analytics or product improvement (no personal identifiers).

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Third-party services & links

Our Services may include third-party integrations (maps, social login, payment gateways). Each third party has its own privacy practices; please review them before use.

6. Data retention

We retain your account information and data for as long as your account is active and necessary to provide you with the Services. We may retain certain information after you deactivate your account for legal or security purposes, or for aggregated analytics.
