Libmatch Help/FAQ

Diperbarui: 13 Januari 2025

1. Quick answers

  • **How do I borrow a book?**
    Search for the title in the app/website, click the 'Borrow' button, and choose Pick-up or Delivery.
  • **For Pick-up:** select a branch/pick-up spot, fill in your entry address and select delivery option. You will receive an email confirmation and notification when the book is available.
  • **What's the difference between Pick-up and Delivery?**
    Pick-up: The book is ready to be collected by you during a selected time window. Usually free. Delivery: we (or a delivery partner) bring the book to your address; delivery fees may apply.
  • **Can I get a refund?**
    Please refer to our Refund Policy and the terms shown during checkout. Contact support for specific cases.

2. Account & Registration

  • **Do I need an account?**
    Yes. An active account helps manage reservations, view borrowing history, and save addresses.
  • **Forgot my password. What do I do?**
    Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen to receive a password reset email.

3. Payments & Fees

  • **What payment methods do you accept?**
    We accept payment methods shown at checkout (credit/debit cards and supported digital wallets). We may offer payment plans, discounts, and store full card numbers.
  • **Are there any hidden fees?**
    No, but late return fees (replacement, late fees) will be shown before you confirm a Borrowing.
  • **What happens if I return the book late?**
    Late fees may apply.

4. Pick-up / Delivery details

  • **How long do I have to pick up my book?**
    You have up to 48 hours after you receive the "Ready for Pick-up" notification. If you miss the slot, we may cancel the loan and apply cancellation rules.
  • **Can someone else pick up the book for me?**
    Yes - when booking Pick-up, include the alternate person's name and bring any required ID during collection.
  • **When is the Delivery / Shipping late?**
    Delivery ETAs are estimates. If your delivery is significantly delayed, contact support and we'll track it with our partners.

5. Returns, damage & lost books

  • **How do I return a book?**
    Return at the specified branch or arrange a Pick-up with our delivery partner if the service is available in your area. Always confirm the return method used.
  • **What if the book is damaged or lost?**
    If a book is lost or returned damaged, charges for damage/replacement will be calculated according to our policy and displayed in your account. Contact support to report issues.

6. Suggestions & Resolutions

  • **I have a suggestion, where can I send it?**
    We love suggestions! Use the "Contact" link in the header or footer to send us an email.
  • **I have a problem that isn't listed here.**
    Please reach out to our dedicated support team through the Contact page.
