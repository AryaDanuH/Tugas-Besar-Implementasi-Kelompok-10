About Libmatch

Diperbarui: 13 Januari 2025

Our mission

To make reading accessible and delightful by helping people discover, borrow, and share books in their community—with convenience, transparency, and respect for creators.

What we offer

  • Easy Borrowing: Search titles, reserve copies, and choose Pick-up or Delivery.
  • Local branches & partners: Borrow from neighborhood libraries, independent bookstores, and community hubs.
  • Flexible periods: Borrow for standard durations with optional extensions (subject to availability).
  • User Community: Create community, view reviews and share reading notes.
  • Secure: Secure payments and clear fee disclosures for deliveries and replacement charges.

Why people choose LibMatch

  • Convenient pickup spots and delivery options.
  • We make returns easy and hassle-free.
  • Respectful of readers and authors—we work with owners and rights-holders.
  • Focused on local communities and discovery.

Our values

  • **Accessibility:** books should be easy to find and borrow.
  • **Privacy:** respecting users' privacy and responsible handling of user data.
  • **Community:** connecting readers with local collections and each other.
  • **Sustainability:** promoting reuse of books over buying new when possible.
