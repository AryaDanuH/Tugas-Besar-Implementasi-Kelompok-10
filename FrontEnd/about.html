About Libmatch

Last Update October 21, 2025

Libmatch is a friendly, modern borrowing platform that connects readers with physical and digital books around them. Whether you like to pick up a paperback from a nearby branch or have a title delivered to your door, Libmatch makes borrowing simple, fast, and affordable.

Our mission

To make reading accessible and delightful by helping people discover, borrow, and share books in their community with convenience, transparency, and respect for creators.

What we offer

  • Easy Borrowing: Search titles, reserve copies, and choose Pick-up or Delivery.
  • Local branches & partners: Borrow from neighborhood libraries, independent bookstores, and partner collections.
  • Flexible periods: Borrow for standard durations with optional extensions (subject to availability).
  • User community: Rate, review, and share reading notes.
  • Safe transactions: Secure payments and clear fee breakdowns for deliveries and replacement charges.

Why people choose Libmatch

  • Convenient pickup slots and delivery options.
  • Transparent fees and clear borrowing rules.

  • Focused on local communities and discovery.

Our values

  • Accessibility: books should be easy to find and borrow.
  • Trust: fair rules, transparent fees, and respectful handling of your data.
  • Community: connecting readers with local collections and each other.
  • Sustainability: promoting reuse of books over buying new where possible.
